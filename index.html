About Me

Always passionate about food and dining from a young age, I got my first job in the food industry at 14 busing tables at a diner on weekends and have been working with food ever since. To refine my cooking skills and knowledge of wines, I moved for a time to Napa CA, where I received a culinary arts degree from the CIA (Culinary Institute of America) and worked in wineries. Since returning to Tucson, it has been a pleasure to share my love for food and wine with individuals, couples, families, and visitors to my desert home.
